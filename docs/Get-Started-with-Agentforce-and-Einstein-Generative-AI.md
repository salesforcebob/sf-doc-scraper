# Get Started with Agentforce and Einstein Generative AI

Agentforce and Einstein bring generative AI to your business at scale. The Einstein Platform with Trust Layer securely connects your data with the power of large language models (LLMs). This guide provides all the information that you need to develop with Agentforce and Einstein Generative AI.

## Develop with Agentforce Agents

Bring the power of conversational AI to your business with Agentforce agents. Build a trusted and customizable AI assistant that empowers your users to get more done in Salesforce.

See [Get Started with Agents](/docs/einstein/genai/guide/get-started-agents.html).

> **Tip:**
> 
> Looking for information about building agents with **Agent Script**? See [Get Started with Agent Script](/docs/einstein/genai/guide/agent-script.html).

## Develop with Models and Prompts

The Einstein Platform provides you with some powerful tools to configure and use large language models (LLMs). You can also use Prompt Builder features to create, manage, and use your prompt templates.

See [Get Started with Models and Prompts](/docs/einstein/genai/guide/models-get-started.html).
